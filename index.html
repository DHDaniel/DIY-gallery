Build Your Own Gallery

About me

For this final project, we are to make a website about ourselves - something that interests us. What interests me is coding, and computer science. So, I have decided to make a website that allows you to make your own interactive gallery, with the photos that you choose.

Note: with some images, when you click on them in the thumbnail or go to them using the buttons, the image takes time to load. The slideshow might also not be smooth. This is because the server where the image is hosted is not allowing for the image to be cached in the browser, and is downloading it every time you click on it. If this happens, please try with other images.
